> [!IMPORTANT]
>Existing customers from before July 1, 2025 can use Microsoft Sentinel in the Azure portal, or onboard to the Microsoft Defender portal for a unified security operations (SecOps) experience. New customers onboarding after July 1, 2025, with permissions of a subscription [Owner](/azure/role-based-access-control/built-in-roles#owner) or a [User access administrator](/azure/role-based-access-control/built-in-roles#user-access-administrator), have their workspaces automatically onboarded to the Defender portal, and use [Microsoft Sentinel in the Defender portal](../microsoft-sentinel-defender-portal.md) only.
